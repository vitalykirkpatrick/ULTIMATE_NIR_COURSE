# Lesson 04 Discussion: Benchtop vs. Portable

**Prompt:**

This lesson compared benchtop and portable NIR instruments. 

*   **Imagine you are a QC manager for a large pharmaceutical company. You can only choose ONE type of instrument for your entire company. Would you choose a high-performance benchtop instrument for your central lab, or a fleet of portable instruments for use in the warehouse and on the production line?**
*   **What are the key factors that would influence your decision?**

There is no single right answer. Justify your choice and discuss the trade-offs.
