# Further Reading: More Than Just a Lab Coat

### On Data Integrity and ALCOA+

1.  **FDA - Data Integrity and Compliance With Drug CGMP:** A comprehensive guidance document from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration. While focused on pharmaceuticals, the principles are universally applicable.
    - [Link to FDA Guidance](https://www.fda.gov/regulatory-information/search-fda-guidance-documents/data-integrity-and-compliance-drug-cgmp-questions-and-answers)

2.  **PIC/S - Good Practices for Data Management and Integrity in Regulated GMP/GDP Environments:** An excellent document from the Pharmaceutical Inspection Co-operation Scheme that provides detailed examples of data integrity controls.
    - [Link to PIC/S Guidance](https://picscheme.org/en/publications)

### On the Role of the Modern Analyst

3.  **"The Analytical Chemist as a Problem Solver" - Journal of Chemical Education:** An article discussing the evolving role of the analytical chemist beyond routine measurements.
    - (Search for this title on Google Scholar or the Journal of Chemical Education website for access options)

4.  **"Mindset: The New Psychology of Success" by Carol S. Dweck:** While not specific to analytical chemistry, this book is essential reading on developing a growth mindset, which is critical for the problem-solving aspects of an analyst's role.
