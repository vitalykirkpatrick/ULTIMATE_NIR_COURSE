# Cheat Sheet: More Than Just a Lab Coat

## The Analyst's Role: 4 Connections

- **R&D:** Translate science to routine methods.
- **Production:** Provide real-time data for decisions.
- **QA:** Gatekeeper of quality and compliance.
- **Management:** Turn data into business insights (ROI).

## ALCOA+ Principles

- **A**ttributable (Who? When?)
- **L**egible (Can you read it?)
- **C**ontemporaneous (Recorded at the time)
- **O**riginal (First record)
- **A**ccurate (Correct, validated)
- **+**
- **C**omplete (All data, including re-tests)
- **C**onsistent (Follows GDP)
- **E**nduring (Safe from loss)
- **A**vailable (Accessible for review)

## The Data Flow

**Sample → Instrument → Raw Data → Information → Insight → Action**

## The Golden Rule

**GARBAGE IN = GARBAGE OUT**

Your model is only as good as your worst sample. Prioritize sample preparation above all else.
