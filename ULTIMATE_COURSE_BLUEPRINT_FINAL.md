## 3. The Course-Long Capstone Project

Students receive a real-world dataset (100 wheat samples with NIR spectra and reference chemistry) at the start. Each module advances the project:

- **Module 1:** Identify spectral features
- **Module 2:** Verify reference data quality
- **Module 3:** Pre-process and explore data
- **Module 4:** Build, validate, and troubleshoot models
- **Module 5:** Present findings and business case

---

## 4. The "12 Deliverables" Standard

Every lesson must include:

1. Slides (HTML, 9-slide structure)
2. Lesson Script (Full narration)
3. Quiz (10 questions)
4. Glossary (Key terms)
5. Cheat Sheet (1-page PDF)
6. Hands-On Exercise
7. Case Study
8. Further Reading
9. Website Assets (Hero + Thumbnail)
10. Audio Narration (MP3)
11. Expert Interview (Written Q&A)
12. Capstone Project Assignment
