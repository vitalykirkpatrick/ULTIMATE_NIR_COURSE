# Three-Tier NIR Course Comparison

This document provides a clear comparison of the content distribution across the three tiers of the NIR course system.

| Topic/Module | NIR Fundamentals (32 Lessons) | NIR for Professionals (36 Lessons) | Ultimate NIR Mastery (110 Lessons) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Introduction** | ✅ (History, Spectroscopy, Why NIR) | ✅ (Review) | ✅ (Comprehensive History & Context) |
| **Science of NIR** | ✅ (EM Spectrum, Vibrations, Overtones) | ✅ (Review) | ✅ (In-depth Physics) |
| **Comparative Methods** | ✅ (vs. Wet Chem, vs. Other Spec) | ✅ (Deep Dive & Case Studies) | ✅ (Quantitative Comparison & Selection) |
| **Instrumentation** | ✅ (Components, Types, Selection) | ✅ (Optimization, IPQ) | ✅ (Advanced Instrumentation, Probes) |
| **Chemometrics** | ✅ (Intro to Cal/Val, PCA) | ✅ (PLS, Preprocessing, Troubleshooting) | ✅ (Advanced ML/AI, DOE, Non-Linear) |
| **Method Development** | ❌ | ✅ (Lifecycle, Reference Methods) | ✅ (Full Validation Lifecycle) |
| **Regulatory & Quality** | ❌ | ✅ (Intro to GxP, SOPs) | ✅ (21 CFR 11, VMP, IQ/OQ/PQ, PAT) |
| **Industry Applications** | ✅ (Overview) | ✅ (Case Studies) | ✅ (Deep Dives, Emerging Fields) |
| **Capstone Project** | ❌ | ✅ (Simple Calibration) | ✅ (Complex Simulation Project) |
| **Career & Business** | ✅ (Career Paths) | ✅ (Business Case, ROI) | ✅ (Consulting, Project Management) |
| **Software & Tools** | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ (Excel, Python, R, Templates) |
